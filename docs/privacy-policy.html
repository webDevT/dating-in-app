Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect

We collect the following information when you submit the "Get Started" form:

  • • Your name
  • • Email address
  • • Company name
  • • Application URL
  • • Number of app users

Additionally, we use Google Analytics and Amplitude to track website activity for analytical purposes.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data collected to

  • • Process your application.
  • • Communicate with you about our services.
  • • Improve our website and services through analytics.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties under any circumstances.

4. Your Data Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your data. To do so, please email us with the subject line "Data Deletion." We will process your request and delete your data within 7 days of receiving your email.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and we encourage you to review it regularly.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@datingcore.io.
