How does user onboarding work?
Users see a new section in your app, open it, read about the service, and click "Apply," agreeing to the terms.
Can users under 21 use the service?
No, the service is only available to users aged 21 and older. If you already collect users’ age data, the section will not appear for those under 21.
Do I need to provide technical support to users?
No, we handle all technical support for your users.
Who moderates and verifies user profiles?
Our team takes care of all moderation and photo verification.
How long does it take to integrate the service into my app?
Integration takes no more than 30 minutes with our clear and easy-to-follow documentation.
Are there any limits on the number of users who can access the service?
No, our servers are designed to handle high traffic and unlimited users.
Can I customize the button and section to match my app’s design?
Yes, you can adjust the color scheme to seamlessly fit your app’s branding.
Are there any hidden fees or extra costs for app owners?
No, there are no hidden fees. The only requirement is a minimum payout threshold of $250.
What happens if one of my users violates the service rules?
Their access to the dating section will be blocked, but they can continue using your app as usual.
How do I get paid?
Earnings are calculated monthly, and payments are sent directly to your account after deducting the agreed commission.
